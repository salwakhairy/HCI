Koshari

Cuisine: Egypt

Overall Time: around 65 to 87 minutes from start to finish

4.6 ★★★★☆

Ingredients

For the Koshari:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup brown lentils
  • 1 cup pasta (macaroni or small penne)
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced and fried until crispy (for garnish)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

For the Garlic Vinegar Sauce:

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Lentils: Rinse lentils, cook until tender, then drain.
  2. Cook the Rice: Boil rice until fluffy and tender, then set aside.
