shawrma

Cuisine: Egypt

Overall Time: around 65 to 87 minutes from start to finish

4.6 ★★★★☆

Ingredients

For the shawrma:

  • Thinly sliced meat (usually chicken, beef, lamb)
  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Lettuce or other greens
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic sauce or tahini sauce
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

For the sharwma Sauce:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

For the Tomaia Sauce:

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Place cooked meat on warmed bread, top with shawarma sauce and vegetables.
  2. Roll up the shawarma and serve immediately.
